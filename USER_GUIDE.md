Brain Guard is your cognitive health companion, designed to help you monitor your mental wellness, maintain daily routines, and stay connected with your care team.

## Getting Started

When you open Brain Guard, you'll see the **Dashboard** - your home base for all activities.

## Main Features

### üè† Dashboard
Your central hub showing:
- **Cognitive Score**: Your 7-day average mental wellness score
- **Today's Routines**: How many activities you've completed today
- **Active Alerts**: Important notifications that need your attention
- **Quick Actions**: Large buttons to access all features
- **Recent Activity**: Latest alerts and today's schedule

### üìä Cognitive Monitoring
Track your mental wellness through:
- **Speech Patterns**: How clearly you communicate
- **Typing Speed**: Your keyboard skills and accuracy
- **Activity Level**: Your daily digital interactions

View your trends to see if you're improving, stable, or need attention.

### üîî Alerts & Notifications
Stay informed with:
- **Severity Levels**: Low, medium, high, or critical alerts
- **Alert Management**: Mark alerts as read or resolved
- **Filtering**: View all alerts, unread only, or resolved alerts
- **Detailed Information**: See when alerts were created and what they mean

### üìÖ Daily Routines
Keep track of your daily activities:
- **Medication**: Never miss your medications
- **Meals**: Track your eating schedule
- **Exercise**: Log your physical activities
- **Sleep**: Monitor your rest patterns
- **Social**: Record social interactions

**How to Add a Routine:**
1. Click the "Add Routine" button
2. Choose the type (medication, meal, exercise, sleep, or social)
3. Enter a title (e.g., "Morning medication")
4. Optionally set a scheduled time
5. Add any notes or details
6. Click "Add Routine"

**How to Complete a Routine:**
- Find the routine in your list
- Click "Mark Complete"
- It will show as completed with a timestamp

### üéØ Cognitive Exercises
Keep your mind sharp with fun activities:
- **Memory Games**: Match cards and recall words
- **Puzzles**: Sudoku and number sequences
- **Language Exercises**: Crosswords and vocabulary
- **Attention Games**: Spot the difference and patterns

**How to Do an Exercise:**
1. Browse available exercises
2. Click "Start Exercise" on one you like
3. Read the instructions carefully
4. Complete the exercise
5. Your score and time are automatically saved

### üìπ Video Call
Stay connected with your care team:
- **View Contacts**: See your caregivers and doctors
- **Check Status**: Know who's online and available
- **Make Calls**: Click "Call" to start a video chat
- **Scheduled Calls**: Join upcoming appointments
- **Easy Controls**: Large buttons for mute, video, and hang up

**During a Call:**
- üé§ **Mute Button**: Turn your microphone on/off
- üìπ **Video Button**: Turn your camera on/off
- üìû **End Call Button**: Hang up when finished

- **80-100**: Excellent cognitive health
- **60-79**: Good cognitive health
- **40-59**: Fair - may need attention
- **Below 40**: Concerning - consult your healthcare provider

## Common Tasks

### Daily Routine
1. **Morning**: Check dashboard for today's routines
2. **Throughout Day**: Mark routines as completed
3. **Evening**: Review what you accomplished
4. **Add New**: Create routines for tomorrow

### Weekly Check
1. **Review Monitoring**: Check your cognitive trends
2. **Read Alerts**: Address any new notifications
3. **Do Exercises**: Complete 2-3 cognitive exercises
4. **Video Call**: Connect with your care team

### When You See an Alert
1. **Read Carefully**: Understand what the alert is about
2. **Check Severity**: See if it's urgent
3. **Take Action**: Follow any recommendations
4. **Mark as Read**: Once you've reviewed it
5. **Contact Care Team**: If you have questions

## Getting Help

### If Something Doesn't Work
1. Try refreshing the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Contact your caregiver or healthcare provider

- Healthcare professionals can explain your scores
- Family members can assist with using the app
